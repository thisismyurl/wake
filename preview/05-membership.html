Membership Notes

When does membership run?

The membership year runs from March 1 to February 28. Applications open February 1. Renewals are processed automatically for continuing members unless cancelled by January 15.

Can I race without a boat?

Yes. Associate members may race as crew on any registered vessel. The crew registry on the member portal connects willing crew with boats that need hands. Wednesday evening races fill crew slots the day of.

Junior membership

Sailors under 25 pay 50% of the Associate rate. Junior members have full race and cruise participation rights. The club runs a junior training program in June each year.
